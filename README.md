## 🧠 About Me

AI Consultant & Solutions Architect with **10+ years** in software engineering and **3+ years** leading enterprise AI programs for government and financial sector clients in Qatar and the GCC.

I specialize in building **production-grade AI systems** — from intelligent chatbots and knowledge management platforms to multi-agent orchestration pipelines and knowledge graphs — using **Azure AI Foundry**, **LangChain/LangGraph**, **Neo4j**, and modern full-stack frameworks.

Currently at **Malomatia (Qatar)**, I lead AI delivery across multiple government institutions including the **Supreme Judicial Council (SJC)**, **Qatar Central Bank (QCB)**, **Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MOCI)**, and the **Civil Service and Government Development Bureau (CGB)**.
